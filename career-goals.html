Carl Alexander - Career Goals

This page outlines my aspirations and vision for a future in IT and cybersecurity.

Career Objectives

I am pursuing a career in the Information Technology field with a long-term goal of becoming a cybersecurity analyst or penetration tester. I am passionate about securing digital environments, protecting sensitive data, and staying ahead of evolving cyber threats.

My short-term objective is to apply my education and certifications, such as CompTIA A+, to gain hands-on experience in help desk, networking, or support roles. I am currently expanding my knowledge in cybersecurity tools and threat detection techniques while completing my degree.

Long-Term Goals

  • Earn certifications like CompTIA Security+, CEH, and eventually CISSP
  • Work in a professional IT security environment
  • Contribute to the prevention of data breaches and cybercrime
  • Grow into leadership roles where I can mentor others in the IT field
